About Elgin

Elgin is a town located in Moray, Scotland, with a population of over 25,000 people.

It has a total surface area of 3.66 square miles, and the postcode district is IV30

The town straddles the River Lossie and is located on the A96 route, connecting Aberdeen and Inverness.
